Free Agent Paul Millsap Most Often Linked With Golden State Warriors (8/24/2021)

Paul Millsap, one of the few remaining prominent names in the free agency pool, has been most often linked to the Golden State Warriors
by sources around the league.

The 36 year-old former All-Star has also drawn interest from teams such as the Hawks, Nets, Pelicans, 76ers, and Timberwolves.

Source: Bleacher Report

Stephen Curry Has Agreed on a Four-Year, $215 Million Extension With the Golden State Warriors (08/03/2021)

The former two-time MVP Stephen Curry has re-signed with the Warriors on a four-year, $215 million extension. This marks the first time
a player has signed back-to-back extensions of over $200 million each.

The sharpshooter has just recently come off of an MVP-caliber season, averaging a league-topping 32.0 ppg, 5.5 rebounds, and 5.8 assists.

Source: Adrian Wojnarowski
